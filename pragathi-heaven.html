ABOUT US

Pragathi Heaven is a contemporary residential haven offering trendy homes with state-of-the-art amenities in one of Hyderabad's most sought-after locations. With chic designs, modern conveniences, and impeccable construction, these homes perfectly embody the ideal living experience, fulfilling every aspiration of a dream lifestyle.

SALIENT FEATURES

Sai Pragathi Heaven, located in the heart of Hyderabad, offers the perfect fusion of modern design and luxurious living. With chic architecture, top-tier amenities, and flawless construction, it ensures a lifestyle of comfort and convenience. Strategically situated near schools, hospitals, shopping hubs, and entertainment zones, it’s an ideal choice for those seeking urban living with a touch of tranquility.

SPECIFICATIONS

Structure:

RCC framed structure, first-class brick masonry, wind & seismic-resistant.

SPlastering:

Smooth lime finish (internal), double-coat sand-faced plastering (external).

Doors & Windows:

Teakwood main door, aluminum French doors & windows with float glass.

Flooring:

Vitrified tiles (living, bedrooms, kitchen), anti-skid tiles (bathrooms, utility).

Bathrooms:

Exterior with 2 coats of emulsion paint (ASIAN or equivalent); smooth interior finish with 2 coats of aEWC with flush tank, geyser provisions, high-quality CP fittings, and reputed brands like Kajaria/RAK.

Electrical:

Concealed wiring, AC and geyser outlets, kitchen and TV provisions, MCBs, and reputed fittings.

Painting:

Apex exterior emulsion (external), premier emulsion with roller finish (internal).

Communication:

Telephone and cable TV points in all bedrooms and living room.

Lifts & Plumbing:

Standard lifts, ISI-marked PVC and GI piping, professional plumbing execution.
