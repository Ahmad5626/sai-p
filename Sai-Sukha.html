SPECIFICATIONS

Structure:

RCC frame with anti-termite foundation, lightweight AAC/Cement bricks with 43-grade cement plastering.

Sanitary:

JAQUAR (or equivalent) CP fittings, CPVC and SWR pipes for water and waste lines.

Flooring:

600x600mm vitrified tiles in living areas, granite/marble in common areas, ceramic tiles in balconies and utilities (KAJARIA/RAK or equivalent).

Electrical:

Concealed copper wiring (FINOLEX/VGUARD), power outlets for ACs, kitchen appliances, TV, and audio systems. MCBs and modular switches of reputed brands.

Painting:

Exterior with 2 coats of emulsion paint (ASIAN or equivalent); smooth interior finish with 2 coats of acrylic emulsion paint.

Lifts:

8-passenger lifts with rescue device, V3F energy-efficient system, granite/vitrified tile cladding.

Internet/Telecom:

Internet and cable TV provisions, telephone points in master bedroom and living room, intercom facility for all units.
